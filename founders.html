Nicolas Pascault

Founding Partner

Expertise: transportation, FMCG and financial sectors.

Prior to founding Anassa Capital in 2022, Nicolas served as CEO for the last 5 years and prior to that as CFO of Brunswick Rail, a rail operating leasing company. Before that Nicolas served as CFO of Danone Group in Moscow and senior manager at Ernst & Young in the Audit and Corporate Finance Department.

Nicolas graduated from the Institut d’Etudes Politiques of Paris and holds a Master of Finance degree from the University of Paris II.

Victor Koshkin

Founding Partner

Expertise: transportation, manufacturing and private equity.

Prior to founding Anassa Capital in 2022, Victor served as General Director of rail operating leasing management company - Brunswick Rail Management.

Prior to that, Victor served as CFO of electrical components producer – United Energy Technologies, Deputy Head of Business Development at aluminium producer SUAL, and adviser to shareholders of MDM Industrial Group specializing on investments in metallurgical, coal and fertilizer sectors.

Victor started his career in industrial and media and telecom investment banking groups at Lehman Brothers and JP Morgan in New York.

Victor holds an MBA degree from Harvard Business School in Boston, MA, USA and a BA degree in Economics from Wabash College in Crawfordville, IN, USA.
